Tr uses word libraries for generating random sentences and quote libraries for the sentences

English sentences come from here: https://github.com/dwyl/quotes/blob/master/quotes.json

English words come from here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sindresorhus/mnemonic-words/master/words.json

Spanish sentences come from here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/GianNipitella/SpanishQuotes/gh-pages/javascripts/citas.json

Spanish words come from here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/words/an-array-of-spanish-words/master/index.json

German sentences come from here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stevencaro/quotation/master/german-quotes.json

German words come from here, Tr uses a reduced version: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/creativecouple/all-the-german-words-ascii/master/ascii-woerter.json
